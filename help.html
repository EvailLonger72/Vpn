Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Strawberry VPN

What is Strawberry VPN?

Strawberry VPN is a secure virtual private network service that protects your online privacy and security. It encrypts your internet connection and masks your IP address, allowing you to browse the web anonymously and access geo-restricted content.

How does Strawberry VPN work?

Strawberry VPN works by creating an encrypted tunnel between your device and our secure servers. When you connect to the internet through this tunnel, your data is protected from prying eyes, and your IP address is masked with one from our servers, making it appear as if you're browsing from a different location.

Is Strawberry VPN free to use?

Strawberry VPN offers both free and premium plans. The free plan provides basic VPN protection with access to standard servers in select locations. For enhanced features like specialized gaming and download servers, unlimited bandwidth, and access to all server locations, you can upgrade to our Premium plan.

What devices can I use Strawberry VPN on?

Strawberry VPN is compatible with Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, and Linux. You can use it on computers, smartphones, tablets, and even some routers. Premium users can connect up to 5 devices simultaneously with a single account.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your email address, create a password, select your account type (Free or Premium), and agree to the Terms of Service. Click "Create Account" to complete the registration process.

How do I upgrade to Premium?

You can upgrade to Premium by clicking on the "Upgrade to Premium" button in the sidebar or on your account page. Select your preferred subscription plan (monthly or annual), enter your payment information, and complete the checkout process.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time from your account settings. Your Premium benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the Free plan.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot password?" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How do I connect to a VPN server?

To connect to a VPN server, log in to your account and go to the Dashboard. Select a server location from the list, and click the "Connect" button. You can also connect from the Servers page by selecting a server and clicking "Connect".

Why is my connection slow?

VPN connection speeds can be affected by various factors, including your base internet speed, distance to the server, server load, and network congestion. Try connecting to a server closer to your location or one with lower load. Premium users can also use our specialized Download Boost servers for faster speeds.

What is the Kill Switch feature?

The Kill Switch is a security feature that automatically blocks your internet connection if your VPN connection drops unexpectedly. This prevents your real IP address from being exposed. You can enable or disable this feature in the Settings page.

What protocols does Strawberry VPN use?

Strawberry VPN supports multiple protocols, including OpenVPN, WireGuard, and IKEv2. By default, we automatically select the best protocol for your connection, but you can manually choose your preferred protocol in the Settings page.

What are the benefits of Premium?

Premium users enjoy several benefits, including access to all 50+ server locations, specialized Gaming, Download, and Upload servers, unlimited bandwidth, faster connection speeds, priority support, and the ability to connect up to 5 devices simultaneously.

What are Gaming Servers?

Gaming Servers are specialized servers optimized for low ping and minimal latency, making them ideal for online gaming. These servers are strategically located near major gaming hubs and use optimized routing to provide the best gaming experience.

What are Download Boost Servers?

Download Boost Servers are optimized for high download speeds, making them perfect for streaming, torrenting, and downloading large files. These servers have enhanced bandwidth allocation and optimized network configurations to maximize download performance.

What are Upload Boost Servers?

Upload Boost Servers are optimized for high upload speeds, making them ideal for content creators, cloud backup, and file sharing. These servers prioritize upload traffic and have optimized configurations to enhance upload performance.

I can't connect to a server. What should I do?

If you're having trouble connecting to a server, try the following steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Try a different server location
  3. Restart the application
  4. Try a different protocol in Settings
  5. Temporarily disable your firewall or antivirus
  6. Clear your browser cache and cookies

If the issue persists, please contact our support team for assistance.

Why can't I access certain websites with VPN?

Some websites and services use VPN detection methods to block access from VPN servers. If you're having trouble accessing a specific website, try connecting to a different server location or using our Stealth mode (available in Settings for Premium users).

My connection keeps dropping. How can I fix this?

Connection drops can be caused by network instability, server issues, or interference from other applications. Try the following solutions:

  • Connect to a server closer to your location
  • Try a different protocol in Settings
  • Check your internet connection stability
  • Disable battery saving modes on mobile devices
  • Ensure no other VPN applications are running

How do I report a bug or issue?

To report a bug or issue, please contact our support team through the Contact page or by emailing support@strawberryvpn.com. Include as much detail as possible, including your device type, operating system, and a description of the issue. Screenshots or error messages are also helpful.

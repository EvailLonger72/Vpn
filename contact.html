Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions below, or visit our Help Center for more information.

How do I connect to a VPN server?

To connect to a VPN server, log in to your account and go to the Dashboard. Select a server location from the list, and click the "Connect" button.

What are the benefits of Premium?

Premium users enjoy several benefits, including access to all 50+ server locations, specialized Gaming, Download, and Upload servers, unlimited bandwidth, and priority support.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time from your account settings. Your Premium benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.
